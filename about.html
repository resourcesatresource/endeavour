WHO ARE WE !

Team Endeavour is a registered technical team of Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology which aims to work in synergy to produce effective solution and innovate in the field of Robotics. The team is comprising of undergraduate and ICD students ranging of all engineering departments sharing a common passion in the field of robotics.

The purpose of the team is to explore new exciting technology, learn critical skills, and promote robotics in SLIET through entering a variety of competition. Endeavour also aims to work on social utility projects apart from competitions. The team prefers to continuously evolve through finding new exciting opportunities and challenges.
